Certify

EcoCertify is a platform that enables users to participate in recycling and donation campaigns, track their impact, and earn rewards for their eco-friendly actions.

How to Certify

Recycle at Vending Machines

Deposit your recyclable items at designated vending machines. Scan the QR code on the machine to log your deposit and earn points.

Recycle at Community Centers

Drop off your recyclables at participating community centers. Provide your EcoCertify ID to the staff to track your contributions.

Recycle at Home

Sort your recyclables at home and schedule a pickup through the EcoCertify app. Our team will collect the items and credit your account.
