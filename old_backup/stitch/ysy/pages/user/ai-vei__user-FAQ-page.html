Donations

Is cash donation possible?
expand_more

Currently, we only accept donations through our partner organizations. Please check our donation page for more details.

Can I get a donation receipt?
expand_more

Yes, donation receipts are available through our partner organizations. You can request one after your donation is processed.

How are donated items used?
expand_more

Donated items are distributed to those in need through our network of charitable partners, supporting various social and environmental causes.

Can donations be rejected?
expand_more

In rare cases, donations may be rejected if they do not meet our partner's guidelines. Please refer to our donation policy for more information.

Points

How do I earn points?
expand_more

You can earn points by participating in various carbon reduction activities, such as using public transport, reducing energy consumption, and more. Check the 'Earn Points' section in the app for a full list of activities.

What can I do with points?
expand_more

You can use your points to purchase eco-friendly products in our shop, or donate them to support environmental projects.

Do points expire?
expand_more

Yes, points expire 12 months after they are earned. We'll notify you before your points are about to expire.
